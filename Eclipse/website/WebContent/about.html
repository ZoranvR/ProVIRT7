About our company

Plaintech started in 1980 as a small server hosting company. Through the years Plaintech has grown and now we are one of market leaders on physical server hosting. Plaintech keeps researching and expanding on the field off server hosting. Since 2014 Plaintech offers virtual server hosting for their customers. With our years of experience we are offering a stable and secure environment for your physical/virtual servers!

Our mission:
Plaintech delivers the best solution when it comes to server hosting. We focus on the best mix of the desire of the customer, available budget and best technical solution. Therefore we deliver the knowledge and advise to realise this mission.
